## PHASE 1: Immediate (This Week)

### 1. Product Hunt Launch
- Create account at producthunt.com
- Prepare assets:
  - Tagline: "Free online tools that just work — no signup, no limits"
  - Description: 10 free productivity tools (invoice generator, JSON formatter, QR codes, etc.)
  - Gallery: screenshots of each tool
  - First comment: your story/why you built it
- Best launch day: Tuesday or Wednesday
- Launch at 12:01 AM PST (peak visibility)

### 2. Reddit Posts
Post to these subreddits (follow each sub's rules, don't spam):

**r/SideProject**
Title: "I built a free online tools site — 10 productivity tools, no signup required"
Content: Brief story + link + list of tools

**r/webdev**
Title: "Free online developer tools — JSON formatter, Base64, Markdown preview, text diff"
Content: Focus on the developer tools specifically

**r/InternetIsBeautiful**
Title: "A clean, fast collection of free online tools — no ads, no signup, no tracking"
Content: Keep it casual, let the site speak for itself

**r/freelance**
Title: "Free invoice generator — create and download professional PDF invoices"
Content: Focus on the invoice tool specifically

**r/SaaS**
Title: "Launched a free tools site — here's my monetization strategy"
Content: Share the affiliate + ads model transparently

### 3. Hacker News
- Submit as "Show HN: QuickTools – Free online productivity tools"
- Be ready to engage in comments

### 4. ghspeaks.com Article
- Write a Ghana-focused article: "10 Free Online Tools Every Ghanaian Freelancer Needs"
- Include QuickTools naturally in the content
- Publish via WordPress
